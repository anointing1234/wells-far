Frequently Asked
Questions

What services are available through your digital banking platform?

Our platform provides secure digital banking capabilities, including account management, fund transfers, payment processing, wallet services, transaction monitoring, and exchange functionality. We do not provide lending, credit, or loan-based products.

How do I register and gain access to the platform?

Registration is completed entirely online. After creating an account and completing the required verification steps, clients gain secure access to their digital banking dashboard and services.

Are fees and charges clearly disclosed?

Yes. We are committed to transparency. All applicable fees are disclosed clearly prior to transaction confirmation, ensuring clients can make informed financial decisions without unexpected charges.
